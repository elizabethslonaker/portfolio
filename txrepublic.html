Texas Republic is a bar and kitchen native to Texas. Located in the heart of Fort Worth's West 7th, they embrace the mantra of everything is bigger in Texas.

02 Market Segmentation

Target Audience

Young adults and millennials between the ages of 21-34, located in the DFW metroplex
Geographics Demographics
  • DFW with a concentration in Fort Worth
  • Suburban to urban
  • Millennial, Young Adults
  • Men & Women
  • High smart phone and internet usage
Behavior Psychographics
  • Aptitude to buy online
  • Technologically competent
  • Highly active on social media
  • Motivated to click on entertaining/funny content
  • Concerned with identity and individualism
  • Prioritizes experiences and capturing memories
  • Seeks instant gratification
  • Enagages with pop culture and trends
What does this mean for the website?
  • Emphasize the Texas culture, specifically Fort Worth
  • Mobile first approach to design and development
  • Ensure booking and contact information is easy to find - reduces bounce rate (SEO)
  • Take advantage of social trends and pop culture (polaroids, neon signs, etc.)
  • Images need to emphasize the Texas Republic "experience" over the products and decor. The products are an important revenue stream, but it's the experience that will ensure brand loyalty.

06 Visual Design
"The Modern Cowboy" Stylescape

I designed a stylescape to identify the site's visual tone and language. Titled "The Modern Cowboy," this stylescape captures the Western culture of Fort Worth, with a modern touch that caters to the young adult target market. The dark tones and neon lights are intended to embody the vibrant nightlife of Texas Republic, while the distressed text and wood textures convey the rustic, weathered charm of Texas.

Online booking decreases customer frustration with being unable to reach a staff member by phone, a key issue discovered in employee interviews. Eliminating current customer frustration with booking will facilitate online sales. Furthermore, Texas Republic can systematically and efficiently track all bookings.
